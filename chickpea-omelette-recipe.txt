Chickpea Omelette Recipe

- 1/3 cup besan chickpea flour
- 7/8 cup egg whites
- 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tbsp freshly minced/shredded ginger root
- 1/4 cup chopped green pepper
- 1/8 cup chopped red onions

Mix all above ingredients together in a bowl. Fry in approx 1 tsp unsalted butter on both sides.

- 1 cup chopped mushrooms

Fry mushrooms in 1.5 tbsp oil. Add mushrooms inside omelette. Fold omelette and serve.
